Top Attractions.

Discover the most beautiful and interesting places in Hyderabad city.

Charminar

The Charminar is a historic-mosque and monument built in 1591 by Muhummad Quli Qutb Shah to comemorate the end of deadly plague. Open daily from 9am to 5:30pm.

Golconda-Fort

Golconda fort is a historic fortress in Hyderabad,India,originally a mud-fort that the Qutub Shahi kings expanded into a massive granite structuire by the 17th century. Open daily from 9am to 5pm.

Birla-Mandir

Birla Mandir is a Hindu temple built on a 280 feet high hillock called Naubath Pahad also known as “Mohabat Pahad” in early days on a 13 acres plot in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. The construction took ten years and was opened in 1976 by Swami Ranganathananda of Ramakrishna Mission. Open daily from 7am to 12pm and 3pm-9pm

Salar Jung Museum

The Salar Jung Museum is an art museum located at Dar-ul-Shifa, on the southern bank of the Musi River in the city of Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It is one of the notable National Museums of India.Open daily from 10am to 5pm

Nehru Zoological Park

Nehru Zoological Park is a zoo located near Mir Alam Tank in Bahadurpura, Telangana, India. It is one of the most visited destinations in Hyderabad.You can see wide varieties of animals and birds etc..Open daily from 8:30am to 4pm

Shri Jagannatha Swami Temple,Hyd

The Jagannath Temple in Hyderabad, India, Telangana, is a modern temple built by the Odia community of the city of Hyderabad dedicated to the Hindu God Jagannath. The temple located near Banjara hills Road no.12 in Hyderabad is famous for its annual Rathyatra festival attended by thousands of devotees.Open daily from 6am to 12pm and 5pm to 9pm

Ramoji Film City

Ramoji Film City is an integrated film studio facility located in Hyderabad, India. Spread over 2,000 acres, it is recognized as the world's largest film studio complex by the Guinness World Records.Open daily from 9am to 5:30pm.

Hussain Sagar-Lake

Hussain Sagar is a heart-shaped lake in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, built by Ibrahim Quli Qutb Shah in 1562. It is spread across an area of 5.7 square kilometres and is fed by the River Musi. A large monolithic statue of the Gautama Buddha, erected in 1992, stands on Gibraltar Rock in the middle of the lake.

Durgam-Cheruvu

Durgam Cheruvu, also known as Raidurgam Cheruvu, is a freshwater lake located in Rangareddy district, Telangana, India. The lake, which is spread over 83 acres, is located near the city of Hyderabad.The Durgam Cheruvu Cable Bridge is an iconic, modern cable-stayed bridge in Hyderabad, connecting Jubilee Hills and the Financial District to alleviate traffic congestion in the city's IT corridor.

Shilparamam Art & Craft-Village

The rural museum, surrounded by trees, is a miniature depiction of a typical Indian village. Over 15 life-sized huts, authentically constructed out of baked clay and thatch, depict rural and tribal lifestyles and the life of various artisans. It provides a window to rural life for city dwellers and those who have never visited a rural village. The museum houses sculptures and life size models depicting the day-to-day activities of the rural artisans.Open daily from 10:30am to 8:30pm

Chilkur Balaji Temple

Chilkur Balaji Temple is an ancient Hindu temple of Lord Balaji on the banks of Osman Sagar in Rangareddy District in Telangana. It is one of the oldest temples in the Hyderabad Dist earlier now in Rangareddy Dist, built during the time of Madanna and Akkanna, the uncles of Bhakta Ramadas. C.S. Open daily from 6am to 1pm and 4pm to 7pm

Qutub Shahi Tombs

The Qutub Shahi tombs are a series of Islamic tombs and mosques built by the various kings of the Qutub Shahi dynasty, located in the Ibrahim Bagh, close to the famous Golconda Fort in Hyderabad, in the Hyderabad district of the state of Telangana, India.Open daily from 9:30am to 5:30pm
