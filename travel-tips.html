Travel Tips.

Essential advice to make your visit enjoyable and stress-free.

Best Time to Visit ~

The ideal time to visit Hyderabad city is during between November to February as the weather is pleasant,days are sunny and evenings are breezy.

Getting Around ~

Hyderabad city has an excellent public transportation system including buses,autos and a metro-rail facility. Consider purchasing a Day-pass travel card for unlimited rides during your stay.

Local Customs ~

When visiting religious sites, dress modestly and remove your shoes when required. Tipping 10-15% is customary in restaurants if service charge isn't included.

Money Matters ~

The local currency is widely accepted,Credit and Debit cards are accepted and UPI is also accepted in many places. It's always good to have some cash for small purchases.

Safety Tips ~

Hyderabad city is generally safe, but be aware of pickpockets in crowded areas. Keep your belongings secure and avoid poorly lit areas at night.

Local Cuisine ~

Don't miss trying our local specialties. Ask locals for recommendations on the best places to eat authentic dishes away from tourist areas.
